Choose Your Wedding Dress!

Are you getting married? Isn't it hard to find the perfect dress to wear on your wedding day?

Well, you've come to the right place! Let me showcase some of my favorite traditional dresses that you can wear on your special, cultural, and unique wedding day.

Image Culture Description
Filipiniana Inspired Wedding Dress Filipiniana wedding dresses are inspired by the traditional filipiniana. The traditional filipiniana has exaggerated, big, puffy, sleeves. Those unique dresses are usually an off-white, beige, or cream color. It has the baro/camisa, the blouse, and the saya, the skirt. When these elements are translated to the wedding dress, the wedding dress is an off-white color that has big, puffy sleeves or a shaul to cover the shoulders.
Chinese Traditional Wedding Dress There are different variations of a Chinese tradional wedding dress from different regions and places but they are usually a red gown with embroidery that is gold. The embroidery features a pheonix or flowers. The dress is really grand and magnificent. But even though the traditional color is red, it is still beautiful if your desired color is white.
Japanese Traditional Wedding Dress A Japanese traditional wedding dress may be pure white or colorful. The shiromuku is pure white with embroidery. The iro-uchikake is a brightly colored robe, usually red as the main color, with gold and silver embroidery.
Indian Traditional Wedding Dress An Idian traditional wedding dress is usually a lehenga or saree. The lenhenga has a heavy skirt, the choli, a cropped or long top, and the dupatta which is worn on the shoulders. The saree, on the other hand, is unstitched and long enough(five to nine yards) to cover the body and tuck into an underskirt. Indians don't usually wear white like Western wedding sincce for them, it symbolizes mourning. So instead, the wear vibrant colors like red, the most traditional, gold, brown, pink, yellow, and many other colors.
Taiwanese Traditional Wedding Dress Taiwanese brides usually have many outfits to change into for different parts of the ceremony. The Taiwanese tradition dress is like the Chinese traditional dress. The qipao usually has a mandarin collar and other element like embroidery and beads. The embroidery features a pheonix or dragon.The dress is usually red with blue accents but it can also be other colors.
